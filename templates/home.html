Understanding the Core Principles of this App

What does this App track? and why?

  1. Sleep Cycles: Sleep occurs in 90-minute cycles. Tracking sleep in multiples of 1.5 hours can help users wake up feeling more rested.
  2. Morning Sunlight Exposure: Exposing oneself to sunlight early in the day helps set the circadian rhythm.
  3. Caffeine Intake: Delay coffee intake by 90â€“120 minutes after waking to avoid energy crashes later.
  4. Exercise: Timing and type of exercise (strength vs. cardio) affect energy levels and sleep quality.
  5. Evening Light Management: Reducing blue light exposure in the evening helps the body prepare for sleep.
